What is Rescrafted?

Rescrafted is a unique online platform designed to connect individuals and businesses who are passionate about recycling. Our platform helps scrap sellers dispose of recyclable materials, buyers acquire these materials, and creators transform them into beautiful art and products. We are committed to reducing waste and making a positive impact on the environment.

Handicrafts Made from Recycled Scrap

Our platform also features an online marketplace where you can find unique handicrafts made from recycled materials. These one-of-a-kind items are handcrafted with care, giving new life to materials that would otherwise go to waste. By supporting this marketplace, you're not only buying beautiful art but also contributing to the circular economy.

The Circular Economy of Rescrafted

Rescrafted operates on a circular economy model, where materials are constantly reused and recycled. Our platform facilitates this cycle by bringing together scrap sellers, buyers, and creators, allowing materials to be recycled, reused, and transformed into new products, reducing waste, and promoting sustainability.

How Rescrafted Works

Our platform connects three key groups: scrap sellers, buyers, and artists/creators. Here's how it works:

  • Sellers: Register as a seller to list recyclable materials like paper, metal, plastic, and glass.
  • Buyers: Browse the available materials, buy items for personal use, or to create something new.
  • Creators: Use recycled materials to create beautiful products and art, then sell them on our marketplace.

Who Can Join Rescrafted?

Our platform is designed for a variety of users:

  • Scrap Sellers: Individuals or businesses who want to sell recyclable materials.
  • Buyers: People or companies interested in purchasing scrap for reuse, crafting, or recycling.
  • Artists and Creators: Designers, makers, and artists who transform scrap materials into new products.
